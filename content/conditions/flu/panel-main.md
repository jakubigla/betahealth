## Check if you have flu 

Flu symptoms come on very quickly and can include: 

- a sudden fever - a temperature of 38C or above
- aching body
- feeling tired or exhausted  
- dry, chesty cough
- sore throat
- headache
- difficulty sleeping
- loss of appetite
- diarrhoea or tummy pain
- nausea and being sick
